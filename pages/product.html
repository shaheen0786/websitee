Butter Tea

Butter tea, also known as "po cha" or "gur gur cha", is a traditional beverage that is popular in the Indian region of Ladakhp, as well as in Tibet and Bhutan. It is made by churning tea leaves, yak butter, salt and water together until a frothy mixture is formed.
